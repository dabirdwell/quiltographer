## 🏭 Understanding Longarm Quilting
Longarm machines are professional equipment ($10K-$30K+) used for:
- **Edge-to-edge quilting**: Repeating patterns across entire quilt
- **Custom quilting**: Freehand or guided artistic designs
- **Pantograph patterns**: Following paper patterns
- **Computer-guided quilting**: Automated pattern execution
