Cutting Instructions

page 4
Step 1: Gather:
one 2-1/2” Fabric B square
three 2-1/2” Fabric C squares
five	2-1/2”	Fabric	D	squares
nine 2-1/2” Fabric E squares
thirteen 2-1/2” Fabric F squares
sixteen 2-1/2” Fabric G squares
two 2-1/2” x 10-1/2” Fabric H rectangles
eight 2-1/2” x 12-1/2” Fabric H rectangles
Step 2:	Arrange	the	squares	and	rectangles	in	five	rows	as	shown.	 
Sew the pieces together to form rows, then press.  
Nest the seams and sew the rows together. Make two side outer borders. 
Step 3: Gather:
two 4-1/2” Fabric B squares
four 2-1/2” Fabric B squares
eight 2-1/2” Fabric C squares
ten 2-1/2” Fabric D squares
ten 2-1/2” Fabric E squares
eleven 2-1/2” Fabric F squares
thirteen 2-1/2” Fabric G squares
two 2-1/2” Fabric H squares
two 2-1/2” x 4-1/2” Fabric H rectangles
four 2-1/2” x 11-1/2” Fabric H rectangles
one 2-1/2” x 12-1/2” Fabric H rectangle
Assemble the Outer Borders
Step 4: Sew a 2-1/2” Fabric B square, 2-1/2” Fabric C square, 2-1/2” Fabric 
D square, 2-1/2” Fabric E square, 2-1/2” Fabric F square, and 2-1/2” Fabric 
G square together in a row, then press. 
Step 5: Sew a 2-1/2” Fabric C square, 2-1/2” Fabric D square, 2-1/2” Fabric 
E square, 2-1/2” Fabric F square, 2-1/2” Fabric G square, and 2-1/2” Fabric 
H square together in a row, then press. 
Step 6: Sew the Step 4 row to the top of the Step 5 row, then press. 
Sew a 4-1/2” Fabric B square to the left side as shown. Press. 

page 5
Step 8: Sew the Step 7 row to the bottom of the Step 6 row, then press. 
Step 9: Repeat Step 4-8 to create a second unit, but this time reverse 
the order of the fabrics sewn to create a mirrored unit. 
Step 10: Sew the Step 8 and Step 9 units to either end of the 6-1/2” x 12-1/2” Fabric H 
rectangle, then press. 
Step 11: Sew a 2-1/2” Fabric C square, 2-1/2” Fabric D square, 
2-1/2” Fabric E square, 2-1/2” Fabric F square, 2-1/2” Fabric G 
square, and 2-1/2” x 11-1/2” Fabric H rectangle together in a 
row, then press. 
Step 12: Sew a 2-1/2” Fabric D square, 2-1/2” Fabric E square, 
2-1/2” Fabric F square, 2-1/2” Fabric G square, 2-1/2” x 11-1/2” 
Fabric H rectangle, and 2-1/2” Fabric G square together in a 
row, then press. 
Step 13: Sew the Step 11 row to the top of the Step 12 row,  
then press. 
Step 14: Repeat Steps 11-13 to create a second unit, but  
this time reverse the order of the fabrics sewn to create a 
mirrored unit. 
Step 15: Sew a 2-1/2” Fabric G square to the top of a 2-1/2” Fabric F square, then press. 
Step 16: Sew the Step 13 and 14 units to either side of the Step 15 unit. Press. 
Step 7: Sew a 2-1/2” Fabric B square, 2-1/2” Fabric C square, 2-1/2” 
Fabric D square, 2-1/2” Fabric E square, 2-1/2” Fabric F square, 2-1/2” 
Fabric G square, and 2-1/2” x 4-1/2” Fabric H rectangle together in a 
row, then press. 

page 6
Step 17: Sew the Step 10 unit to the top of the Step 16 unit. Press to complete the top border. 
Repeat Steps 3-17 to make the bottom border.
Assemble the Quilt
Step 18: Sew the side panel 
borders to the sides of the 
Fabric A rectangle. Press 
toward the borders. Sew the 
top and bottom side panel 
borders to the quilt center. 
Press toward the borders. 
Step 19: Sew the side outer 
borders to the sides of the 
quilt center. Press toward  
the borders. Sew the top 
and bottom outer borders  
to the quilt center. Press 
toward the borders. 
Your quilt top is complete! 
Baste, quilt, bind, and enjoy!
