Quiltographer

Layers 1, 2, 3 for Quilt Pattern, Quilt Design (has a source pane showing currently working fabrics and threads), Embroidery or Customization.
Block Library (customizable with add-on packs). Work with quilt historians 

Construction sequence is very important, taking into account where to start, and the order that prevents damage to the machine or design construction problems. 

There is no "Pattern Reader" in the market. People who write the instructions are not good at being clear. An assistant that could walk you intelligently through step by step would be invaluable, especially for folks that suffer from diminishing faculties of vision an hearing. Accessibility accommodation would be a big appeal. 

Ex: Step 4: Attach 1 1/2inch block to 1 1/2 inch block (shows visual example). Something that simple is helpful.
